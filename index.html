Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is a powerful adaptogenic herb known for reducing stress, improving stamina, and boosting immunity. Perfect for modern lifestyle challenges.
Ashwagandha (Withania Somnifera)
Ashwagandha, scientifically known as Withania Somnifera, is one of the most revered herbs in Ayurvedic medicine. This remarkable adaptogen has been used for over 3,000 years to enhance vitality and help the body manage stress.

The herb works by regulating cortisol levels, supporting adrenal function, and enhancing the body's natural ability to cope with physical and mental stressors.

Active Compound

Withanolides

Soil Type

Sandy loam

Duration

150–180 days

Best For

Stress relief

Avoid If

Thyroid disorders

Tulsi

Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is known as the "Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda. It supports respiratory health, boosts immunity, and has anti-inflammatory properties.
Tulsi (Holy Basil)
Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is known as the "Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda. It supports respiratory health, boosts immunity, and has anti-inflammatory properties. This sacred herb is widely grown for both medicinal and spiritual purposes.

Active Compound

Eugenol

Soil Type

Loamy, well-drained

Duration

60–90 days

Best For

Respiratory health

Avoid If

On blood-thinners

Cardamom

A fragrant spice known for its sweet, slightly citrusy flavor and strong antioxidant properties. It supports digestion and freshens breath.
Cardamom
Cardamom, often called the "Queen of Spices," is valued for its aromatic pods. It promotes digestive health, reduces inflammation, and has antimicrobial properties. Traditionally used in Ayurveda, it supports respiratory health.

Active Compound

Cineole

Soil Type

Rich loamy soil

Duration

2–3 years

Best For

Digestive health

Avoid If

Gallstones

Clove

Cloves are rich in antioxidants, help fight bacteria, reduce inflammation, and support digestion.
Clove (Syzygium aromaticum)
Clove contains eugenol, which provides pain relief and fights microbial infections. Traditionally used for toothaches, it also supports metabolic health and protects cells from oxidative damage.

Active Compound

Eugenol

Soil Type

Well-drained loamy

Duration

6–8 years

Best For

Oral health, pain relief

Avoid If

Bleeding disorders

Cinnamon

A natural antioxidant, helps control blood sugar, supports heart health, and reduces inflammation.
Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum)
Containing cinnamaldehyde, cinnamon supports diabetes management, reduces cholesterol, and protects against oxidative stress. It’s used for digestive issues and may offer neuroprotective benefits.

Active Compound

Cinnamaldehyde

Soil Type

Sandy loam

Duration

2–3 years

Best For

Blood sugar control

Avoid If

Liver problems

Turmeric

Turmeric has powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, supports liver and heart health, and aids digestion.
Turmeric (Curcuma longa)
Curcumin, turmeric’s active compound, is known to reduce inflammation, improve arthritis symptoms, protect the liver, and boost brain function. It may help prevent chronic diseases and support overall wellness.

Active Compound

Curcumin

Soil Type

Fertile loamy soil

Duration

7–9 months

Best For

Arthritis relief

Avoid If

Gallbladder disease
